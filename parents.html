Who it's for

The conflicted parent — not the convert

This isn't for parents who are already all-in on AI. It's for the ones who tried it once, hit a wall, and gave up — or who are still figuring out whether to trust it at all.

The burned parent

"I tried it. It forgot everything overnight."

You set up a chat with your family info, used it for a week, and came back to find it had no memory of any of it. That's a real product limitation, not user error — and there's a specific fix that most people never find because it's buried in a settings menu. That fix is the first thing this course covers.

The skeptic

"I'm not anti-AI. I just haven't seen it earn its place."

You have a healthy distrust of hype. You're not looking for someone to convince you AI is amazing — you're looking for someone to show you what it actually does, in plain language, without the sales pitch. My wife built this course alongside me for exactly that reader. She came in skeptical too.
